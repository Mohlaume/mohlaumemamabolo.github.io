About

Mohlaume is a Johannesburg-based multimedia designer, specializes in Branding, Graphic Design, Web Design, 2D & 3D Illustration, visual effects,
Videography and founder of 62 Visuals,my skills broaden much further into all aspects of design; I'm an all round creative as they might say.
Throughout my career, I have always made an effort to ensure that my enthusiasm and passion for my work have shown through, despite my modest
successes as an Designer. I have a strong passion for bringing my clients' visions to life and for being creative.
